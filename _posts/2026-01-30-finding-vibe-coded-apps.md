Actually, I'd love to be able to find an app from ready-made ones, but unfortunately Google sucks at this. Searching GitHub can also take half an hour. Plus you need to download something, install it, compile it, run it, and figure out what the author was trying to say.

But there's no way to search like this. And it seems fundamentally impossible. I thought about making some kind of catalog for vibe-coded apps, but I imagine search would be just as big a problem there.
