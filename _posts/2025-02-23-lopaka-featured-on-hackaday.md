They wrote about me on Hackaday! It's a popular resource among electronics enthusiasts.

Turned out to be a really nice review of [lopaka.app](https://lopaka.app). They described all the main features, gave praise, and noted that the free version is pretty generous.

They also wrote that it's missing a code editor. And that's the most requested feature. But I can't figure out how to make it convenient. And none of the users have been able to explain what exactly they want. Any ideas?

Also posted the link on Hacker News: [https://news.ycombinator.com](https://news.ycombinator.com)
If you hang out there often, please go to the New section and upvote: Graphics and UI editor for electronic projects (hackaday.com)

So far this is the most successful publication (see screenshot)
1. Post on a niche subreddit with 800K members—80K views
2. Mention and link in a YouTube video—250K views
3. Post on a niche news blog — web (?) + social media (250K on Twitter)

All of this was free.

I had the opposite expectations: thought Reddit was the bomb and sites were trash. Turns out it's completely the opposite.
