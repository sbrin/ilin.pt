Everyone keeps saying that artificial intelligence is killing artists, killing creators, and all that

But I'm more drawn to the opinion that AI kills those who **stopped being creators**.

This is a very powerful catalyst for change. Like in any crisis: some adapt and use it to leap forward, while others, unfortunately, don't.

Some slide down and get stuck in the same old routine, and it grinds them up.

A huge wave is coming. And we have a choice — to become a master who knows how to work with this wave, to make it swirl around you.

That is the cutting edge of art right now.

I don't have a drop of fear for the new things coming into our lives.

When chaos breaks through, it increasingly shows its true face through the cracks in old structures that past generations imposed on us. Adapting to new technologies is understanding the "algorithms" of how consciousness plays.

Consciousness dwells in bliss within its own wildness and desire to resist everything. And we are the epicenter of these changes. In old clothes, in old "spacesuits," we won't master these new spaces. We need to create new identities.
